Our Story

Built from dedication, trust, and long-term industrial partnerships.

Founded on November 13, 1989, CAPP Industries, Inc. started with procurement contracts and fabrication of rubber moldings for suppliers in the mining and beverage industries.

Through years of hard work, the company evolved into supplying cement, heavy equipment, vessel-chartering, hauling, and raw materials to cement facilities across the Philippines.

Business Overview
Business Overview

Expanding from construction support to nationwide raw material supply.

CAPP ventured into the construction industry in 1991. At the onset, it formed a mutually beneficial relationship with global beverage leader Coca-Cola Bottling Corporation and Coca-Cola Exports.

CAPP also began constructing plants and renovating offices across Luzon, the economic and political hub of the Philippines.

Through dedicated service and excellent performance, CAPP earned the trust of its clients and grew into engineering and equipment support, including supplying one million bags of cement for the EDSA Metro Rail Transit Project.

With its growing experience, CAPP recognized the strong potential of supplying raw materials to the nation’s cement facilities. Today, CAPP continues to serve as one of the country’s principal suppliers of bulk raw materials to local and multinational cement manufacturers.

CAPP Industries Inc. was awarded the rights to distribute raw and treated gypsum across the nation by the Philippine Phosphate Fertilizer Corporation.
